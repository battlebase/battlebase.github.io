Battle Points

Battle Points are the in-game currency used in PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds. You receive it when the match ends. The amount varies depending the amount of kills you have made, how long you managed to survive and and the total damage dealt to other players.

Points for Winning:

Solo: 820 BP

Duo: 400 BP

Squad: 20 BP

Points per Kill:

Solo: 20 BP

Duo: 15 BP

Squad: 10 BP

Squad: 10 BP

Points per Damage:

Approx. 1BP per 5 damage BP
