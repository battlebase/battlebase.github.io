GameModes
Solo

It's you against all, be the last one to survive.

This mode is the one that most reward Battle Points (BP).

Solo FPP

Same as solo, except this mode is locked to FPP (First Person Perspective) only.

Available in:

North America (NA) Europe (EU) Asia (AS) Korea/Japan (KR/JP)

Duo

It's you and a friend against other duos.

In this mode if your health reaches zero you will get knocked and start bleeding.

If your health reaches zero after get knocked you die.

Your friends can revive you, however you will be revived with 10 health.

Duo FPP

Same as duo, except this mode is locked to FPP (First Person Perspective) only.

Available in:

North America (NA) Europe (EU) Asia (AS) Korea/Japan (KR/JP) Oceania (OC)

Squad

Up to four players against other teams up to four players.

In this mode if your health reaches zero you will get knocked and start bleeding.

If your health reaches zero after get knocked you die.

Your friends can raise you, however you will be raised with 10 health.

Squad FPP

Same as squad, except this mode is locked to FPP (First Person Perspective) only.

Available in:

North America (NA) Europe (EU) Asia (AS) Korea/Japan (KR/JP) Oceania (OC) South and Central America (SA) South East Asia (SEA)
