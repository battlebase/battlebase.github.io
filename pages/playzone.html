The Play Zone

The Play zone, also known as "The Circle," is the area where players must remain during the game.

If outside of the playzone limiter (Blue Circle), players take damage. This forces players closer to each other as the game progresses in order to speed up the game.

Always try to stay inside the Play Zone (White circle) to avoid taking damage.

Damage Outside The Play Zone

After 3 minutes it starts a 15 second countdown, then it takes 15 more seconds to go completely closes.

Reviving Teammates

Circle 4, you can survive for 5 seconds - the player helping you will take 10% damage while helping you. Circle 1-4, help your buddies.

Circle 5, you can survive for 2 ticks after being helped up, so you'd have to be close to to entering the circle.

Circle 6-8, do not help your downed mates outside the play area.
